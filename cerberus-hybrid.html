Dear Colleagues,

On Friday, February 3, UTFA is hosting a special public event, and all UTFA members are invited to attend.

Professor Mohamad Tavakoli (U of T) will moderate a special panel featuring Professors Homa Hoodfar (Concordia) and Payam Akhavan (McGill), who will discuss intellectual freedom and its international importance.

UTFA contributed to efforts to expedite Professor Hoodfar's release from solitary confinement in Iran's Evin prison, where she was held for 112 days in 2016 on accusations of "dabbling in feminism." She was released on September 26, 2016. Professor Hoodfar teaches and researches development, culture and gender in the Middle East.

Professor Akhavan teaches international criminal law and dispute settlement, human rights and cultural pluralism. Having appeared as counsel and advocate in leading cases before the International Court of Justice, the International Criminal Court and the Permanent Court of Arbitration, in 2016 he was appointed a Member of the Permanent Court of Arbitration (PCA) in The Hague.

The event will take place on Friday, February 3, from 3:30 to 6:30pm at the U of T Earth Sciences Auditorium, 5 Bancroft Ave.
